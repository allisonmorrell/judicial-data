Judges Appointed between 2007 and 2017, by gender

These statistics highlight the number of judges appointed between January 1, 2007, and October 27, 2017, according to gender. Please note that these numbers include newly appointed judges as well as those who were appointed or elevated to other courts during the same period (see breakdown below). For example, this would include judges appointed to courts of appeal from the trial level courts.
